Who is Your Target Customer?

Answering “people who pay us money” is a cop out. Describing your customers by simple demographics isn’t good enough. If you’re going after “25-35 year old women in New York City”, your focus isn’t, well, focused enough.

To understand why someone would want what you’re selling, you need to understand who they are.

You need to know:

  1. What are thier pains?
  2. Who do they listen to?
  3. What are they seeking?
  4. What will they pay for?
  5. How do you get their attention?
