Project Phases

  1. Research: It’s important that we gain understanding of the current situation, as well as what needs to happen moving forward, before we begin work. In this phase, we wrap our heads around the project and create the roadmap to get it done (our short-term sessions are great for this phase).
  2. Advise: All the knowledge and insight we gathered in the Research Phase gets compiled into a report. We sit down with your team to go over the plan, mapping out what happens next.
  3. Act: This is where the rubber meets the road. The project starts to take shape and the the plan comes together (we love it when that happens). We work alongside your team, leading the process and ensuring everyone knows what’s happening.
  4. Measure: If we don’t examine the results, there's no way to know if the project accomplished its goals. We schedule regular check-ins to look at data and make sure our efforts paid off.
