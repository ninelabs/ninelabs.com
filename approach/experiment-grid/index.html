Learn to Innovate Through Experimentation

Innovation isn't an accident. If you want different results, you have to try different methods.

You can't solve a problem with the same thinking that created it.

Albert Einstein

Originally created by Alissa Briggs, the Experiment Grid is a great tool to help groups or teams discuss what they want to try and document the results. We've found it to be a helpful tool when disucssing ideas and how to act on them.

Using the Grid

It’s best to draw your own on a whiteboard and use sticky notes, but you can also download and use our PDF template:

Download the PDF

Fill in the answers as you have them, don’t worry about being neat and tidy..

Check Your Work

Ask others to review your grid, make suggestions, and add details or context.
