We Care About Community

We believe the Web should help make the world a better place for everyone. That’s why we’re very involved with the local community.

Part of being a good citizen is helping others. Our way of helping is by providing leadership and resources to the local community through hosting regular meetups, events, and workshops.

We’re fortunate enough to know a lot of great people in this business, so we invite them to come share their knowledge to help bolster the skills of the community.

Atlanta is rapidly becoming the hub for technology and digital creativity of the South, and we’re happy to be helping it get there.

Atlanta Web Design Group

The mission of the Atlanta Web Design Group is to educate and foster the web and technology community in the Atlanta area through events, workshops, and publications; to advance web design and development as a craft; to provide a forum where web professionals will collaborate, communicate, and grow as individuals; and to amplify the voice of web professionals.

The group is now over 3000 members strong and hosts multiple events each month.

Web Afternoon

The AWDG community expressed a thirst for more than single-speaker events, so we created one. Web Afternoon is a single-track event focused on bringing thought leaders to the local community in a setting which encourages interaction and inspiration.

There have been four events in Atlanta, and several others in Charlotte, Charleston, and Augusta.

Polygon

One of the main struggles with any community is finding a stable, low-cost meeting place. Atlanta was no different. A number of groups expressed their need for a good place to have their events, so we opened one.

Polygon is a 2,300 sq. ft. meeting and event space located right in the heart of the city. It has fantastic a audio/video setup, plentiful low-cost parking, and is just a few steps from every amenity you could want.

Since opening in October 2014 we’ve held over 150 events and hosted over 3,000 people, giving the local tech and creative community a place to call home.
