Application Prototyping

When you have an app idea, the urge to launch a caffiene-fueled rush to the app store is strong. But if you’re serious about your business, you need to slow down. Creating and testing a prototype is a key step.

A prototype is a version of your app that you can show to people and test in the leanest (i.e. the least time and budget) way possible. It’s similar to the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) we all know from the “Lean Startup” movement. But unlike a MVP, you’ll be able to test a prototype with real people before putting all the effort (and money) into building the real thing.
