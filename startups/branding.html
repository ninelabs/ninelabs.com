{% include startup-aside.html %}

Your brand is much more than a logo.

A well conceived name and logo will communicate your message and mission in a way words are unable to (remember, a picture is worth a thousand words).

The Value of Great Branding

  1. Your brand serves as the mental bookmark in the minds of the public for your organization and what it does.
  2. It's a reference point and cornerstone for future branding and marketing efforts.
  3. Clear usage guidelines will ensure your brand is used in a consistent manner, which communicates professionalism and trustworthiness to the general public.
  4. Clear usage guidelines simplify the process for all other design projects.

Our brand specialists will work with you to understand and codify the precise message you wish to communicate through your brand identity.

What you’ll get:

  • Strategic Planning and Discovery session with your key stakeholders.
    • We will discuss your goals and ambitions, and how we should best communicate your message to the public. Limited to two hours.
  • Logo ideation in two iterations:
    • Concepting We will show you no more than three concepts and then work with you to discuss the merits of each and decide which one will become the final Logo.
    • Development: We will create the final Logo according to the outcome of the concepting meeting.
  • Creation of Identity Package
    • Final Logo in full-color and black and white versions.
    • Typographic kit including one headline and one body copy typeface.
    • Color palette consisting of no more than three colors.
  • Creation of basic Usage Guidelines Document including:
    • Logo sizing, clear-space, and positioning guidelines
    • Color usage guidelines
    • Typeface usage guidelines
  • Final Delivery Meeting: We will discuss the final Logo and Usage Guidelines Document with your key stakeholders so that you have a solid understanding of what youâ€™ve received and how it should be used.
