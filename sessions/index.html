Consulting / Work Sessions

There are times when you need a little help without a big commitment. We get that, so we offer these simple sessions.

What’s Included

You get full use of our skills to help with your situation. One of our Partners — J, Brad, or Moses (depending on skills required) — will be available to you and your team for the amount of time you choose. At the end of the session all work generated during that session is yours to keep and use however you like.

What can we accomplish?

We can talk about Brand Strategy, User Experience Design and Strategy, User Interface Design, Information Architecture, technical systems integration, project scoping, workflow improvement, application ideation, or whatever you feel we can help you with.

These sessions are a great way to:

  • Help define scope and scale for a new project
  • Jump start a new project
  • Get extra short-term horsepower on an existing project
  • Get a second opinion on something you’re struggling with
  • Simply test out how we work together before committing to a larger project

Rates

Rates depend on how many of our Partners you’d like to have in the session. If you’re looking for something more intense or involved than two Partners for a week, we should talk about a project based or retainer relationship instead.

One Partner

  • Half-Day: $1,295
  • One-Day: $2,495
  • Three Day: $7,495
  • Week: $11,995

Two Partners

  • Half-Day: $2,295
  • One-Day: $4,495

Note: Travel outside the Atlanta Metro area will incur travel fees of $500 per person.
