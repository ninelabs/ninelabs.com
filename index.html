Process

We start by asking questions and doing research. Then we take what we’ve learned about you, sit in a room with mountains of information, a fast Internet connection, and a healthy supply of coffee; and we stay there until the answers are clear.

You’re involved every step of the way. Think of it like a partnership. When you engage us on a project we work with you to create a solution that works for everyone. And we never overextend ourselves. You can be confident that your project is getting the attention it deserves at all times.

1) Discover

We get to know you and your business. We talk with you about your goals and how you got where you are today.

2) Research

We dig into the issues to understand why. By wielding research methods, sources, etc. we sift through the details to find an understanding.

3) Evaluate

Once we understand, we evaluate. Using different testing methodologies, UX testing, and others to assess the best methods to achieve your goals.

4) Recommend

You get a detailed report of what we found, as well recommendations on how to proceed. Then we help you implement changes to get you to your goals.
