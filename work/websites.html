Coming Soon pages just don’t cut it anymore. Your website needs to be up and running to make a good impression. Even if all you have is some basic info about your company and the people who founded it, that’s a signal of credibility your future customers and investors will appreciate.

Tell us what you’re all about and we’ll make a simple and effective website to tell that story to the world.

Basic Microsite

3-5 days / $7,495 to $12,990

Use your existing text and image content to develop the microsite. The results of this option would include, but not be limited to:

  • Create static HTML and CSS, or Squarespace website (no Wordpress) based on pre-existing theme we select for you.
    • Minor theme customizations (add your logo/branding, change colors, text styles)
    • Up to 3 separate pages with up to 1000 words per page
  • You supply all content
    • Text content would be near verbatim as we receive it from you, with minor edits and other corrections (typos, spelling, grammar).
    • Images would be used as supplied, limited cropping and resizing as necessary
  • Deployment to be publically visible.
  • Install tracking mechanism (Google Analytics, Clicktale, Full Story, or similar)
  • Testing and validation
